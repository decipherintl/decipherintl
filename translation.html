Translation

Quality translation capabilities can protect a client reputation, win business, handle legal issues or establish key contacts. Our efficient and cost effective translation work is carried out by qualified in-house translators – meaning clients can be confident of the accuracy and confidentiality of their content. Decipher provides Arabic, Persian and Mandarin translation across most professional disciplines, including:

  • Marketing translation
  • Legal translation
  • Financial translation
  • Technical translation
  • Medical translation
  • On-call in-country or remote interpreters

We guarantee the consistent quality of our translations as each project is a collaboration between two translators – one native in the source language, and the other native in the target language. This ensures that content is both correctly understood and accurately conveyed. Decipher handles translation tasks with the utmost discretion – projects are handled in-house, offering total security for sensitive information.
